About Me

From structural plans to digital platforms — CAD to code

IIT Dhanbad, earlier known as the Indian School of Mines, was started in 1926. It is one of the oldest and most well-known engineering colleges in India. It first became famous for mining and earth science, but now it also offers great courses in many areas of engineering and technology. Studying at such an old and respected college has had a big impact on how I learn and think about my future.

While I focus on construction, infrastructure, and project planning in my course, I've also developed a strong interest in software development and web technologies. This mix of Civil Engineering and tech helps me see problems from both sides. I'm learning frontend tools like HTML, JavaScript, and modern CSS.

My aim is to use technology to improve real-world engineering — whether that's building tools for construction management, creating visuals for engineering work, or developing platforms that make engineering tasks simpler and more efficient.
